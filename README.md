# Green Points 2.o
GreenPoints is an environment-focused citizen engagement platform designed to promote sustainable living. Users perform eco-friendly activities such as tree plantation, cleanliness drives, waste reduction, and public awareness initiatives. By uploading photo-proof and short descriptions, they earn reward points that reflect their contribution to society. The app recognizes top performers, partners with NGOs and local authorities, and motivates communities through gamification, CSR collaboration, and impact-driven analytics.
